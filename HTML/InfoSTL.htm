STL COMMUNICATIONS LTD

STL Communications is a leading provider of business telephone systems, lines, calls and mobiles, providing cost-effective solutions for private and public sector organisations of all sizes. Using innovative communications that harness the latest advances in voice and data technology, STL installs and maintains telephony systems that add real value to an organisation’s communication requirements. STL’s GreenMinutes initiative helps organisations meet their environmental and CSR objectives by offsetting the carbon cost of their calls. GreenMinutes also facilitates smarter working practices with a range of free eco features including free conference calls, free home worker connections and free use of a video conferencing facility as well as online billing and mobile phone recycling. STL is a carbon neutral company and responsibility to the environment is a core value.
