STAR TRAC

Star Trac engages in the manufacture of fitness equipment. It offers treadmills, total body stepper, bikes, instinct, spinners, and specialty racks. The company also offers cardiovascular and strength equipment; and HumanSport, a cable-based strength training equipment. It serves apartments, hospitality industry, military, schools/universities, health clubs, non profit fitness centers, professional sports centers, corporate/wellness/rehab centers, and home fitness centers. The company is based in Irvine, California.
